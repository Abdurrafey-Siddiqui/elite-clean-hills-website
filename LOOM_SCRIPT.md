# 🎥 Loom Script — Elite Clean Hills Website Pitch
**Estimated Duration:** 3.5 – 4 minutes  
**Tone:** Confident, enthusiastic, outcome-focused  
**Screen:** Have the live site open and ready to scroll

---

## 🎬 INTRO (0:00 – 0:25)

> *(Start screen on the full Hero section — above the fold)*

"Hey [Client Name], thanks for taking a few minutes to check this out. I'm going to walk you through the website we've built for Elite Clean Hills — and I think you're going to love what we've put together.

This isn't just a website. This is a **lead-generating machine** designed to turn visitors into booked jobs. Let me show you how."

---

## 💥 HERO SECTION (0:25 – 1:00)

> *(Slowly scroll through the Hero, pointing at key elements)*

"The very first thing a visitor sees when they land on the site is this — a stunning, full-screen split image of a driveway pressure clean transformation. Instantly, they see the *result* — not a list of services, not a paragraph of text. The result.

The headline — *'The Hills' Most Trusted Pressure Cleaners'* — paired with the 5-star Google rating right underneath builds trust immediately.

And right here on the right-hand side? A **Get a Free Quote form** — front and centre, above the fold, so visitors never have to hunt for how to contact you. The form is clean and simple: name, phone, and service needed. That's it.

Underneath the hero we've got your trust indicators — **Compliance Focused, 100% Satisfaction, Eco-Friendly** — the things customers are looking for before they even pick up the phone."

---

## 📊 STATS BAR + ABOUT SECTION (1:00 – 1:30)

> *(Scroll to Stats Bar and About section)*

"As we scroll down, visitors are immediately hit with your numbers: **500+ Homes Restored**, a **5.0 Google Rating**, **100% Satisfaction**, and **24-hour Quote Turnaround**. These are credibility builders that make people stay on the page.

Then we've got your About section — your logo is front and centre, large and proud, and the copy on the right speaks directly to homeowners in The Hills District. It's professional, it's personal, and it builds a real sense of trust. No waffle, no fluff — just the things that matter to your customer."

---

## 🛠️ SERVICES + HOW IT WORKS (1:30 – 2:10)

> *(Scroll through Services and How It Works)*

"Every service you offer — from High-Pressure Cleaning to Commercial & Strata — has its own dedicated card with a rich photo and a clear description. This section helps Google understand what you do, which is great for SEO, and it helps customers quickly find the service they need.

Below that, we've got the *How It Works* section — three dead-simple steps. Instant quote, easy scheduling, professional clean. This removes every objection a customer might have about the process. They don't even need to be home. That's a big deal for busy homeowners."

---

## 💼 PACKAGES (2:10 – 2:35)

> *(Scroll to Packages)*

"Next — the Packages section. This is a conversion powerhouse. We've got three clearly priced bundles, and the middle one — the *Ultimate Property Makeover* — is highlighted as the most popular with a **Save 15%** badge. Pricing psychology at work. Giving people an anchored 'best value' option leads to more high-ticket bookings.

Each package has a clear price, the included services, and a strong call-to-action button."

---

## 🖼️ GALLERY + REVIEWS (2:35 – 3:10)

> *(Scroll to Gallery, drag the before/after slider)*

"Now — this is probably my favourite part of the site. The before-and-after gallery uses an **interactive drag slider**. Visitors can grab that handle and slide it themselves to reveal the transformation. It's deeply engaging, incredibly visual, and it does your sales pitch *for* you.

Then we roll into your **Google Reviews** section — five stars, eighty-plus reviews, and three powerful testimonials from real customers. Marina, Alex, Vanessa — all speaking to your quality, communication, and results. Social proof like this is gold."

---

## 📋 CONTACT FORM + TRUST BADGES + SERVICE AREAS (3:10 – 3:40)

> *(Scroll through Contact, Trust Badges, and Service Areas)*

"The Contact Form here at the bottom gives customers a second opportunity to reach out — with a photo upload option for faster quoting. Super smart for a field-service business.

Right below that, the Trust Badges — Fully Qualified, Police Checked, White Card & EWP, WWCC Certified — the kind of credentials that seal the deal for strata managers and schools.

And finally, the Service Areas map covers everywhere from Castle Hill to Bondi — making it crystal clear just how wide your reach is."

---

## 🚀 CLOSE (3:40 – 4:00)

> *(Scroll back to the very top slowly, letting the site speak for itself)*

"So that's the site. It's designed to do one thing — convert visitors into quotes, and quotes into jobs.

Every section has a purpose. Every design decision is focused on trust, outcomes, and making it as easy as possible for someone to say yes.

We're ready to go live whenever you are. I'll send over the next steps — let me know if you have any feedback or questions."

---

## 📝 PRESENTER NOTES
- Keep cursor movement **slow and deliberate** — let the visuals breathe
- Demo the **before/after gallery slider** yourself on camera — it's the most visually impressive moment
- If screen recording, open site at **1280px wide** for best layout
- Have the **mobile view** ready to show at the end as a bonus if time allows
